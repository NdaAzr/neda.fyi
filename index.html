I am an Assistant Professor in Data Science and AI at the University of Sheffield, Information School. My research focuses on developing computational models using computer vision and multimodal AI to support clinical decision-making.

My expertise spans medical imaging, deep learning, and trustworthy AI. Examples of my work include automated view detection in echocardiography, LV segmentation, speckle tracking, colonic polyp detection, and cancer pathology image analysis. I'm especially focused on the ethical and interpretability aspects of AI in healthcare.
